ZBZ is ZBZ's multilingual personal blog for long-term notes on building, investing, and life.

ZBZ earns revenue through Google AdSense advertising and uses Google Tag Manager to load Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity for traffic and reading-experience measurement. See the [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy-policy/) for details.
