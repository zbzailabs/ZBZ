RealRip is a multilingual personal blog for long-term notes on building, investing, and life.

The site provides article reading, on-site search, RSS feeds, and basic page browsing only. It does not provide accounts, memberships, online transactions, consulting, or user content hosting.

RealRip earns revenue through Google AdSense advertising and uses Google Tag Manager to load Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity for traffic and reading-experience measurement. See the [Privacy Policy](/ko/privacy-policy/) for details.
