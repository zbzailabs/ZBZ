### Weekly Reflections

The wealth that partners can get from the company includes several aspects:

From the perspective of interests:

- Equity realization: When the company is listed or acquired, or when equity is transferred, partners can obtain income by transferring the shares they hold. Generally, when a company is established, the shares are one yuan per share. As the company develops, the assets corresponding to each share will increase. At this time, transferring shares will obtain a certain premium.
- Dividends: Companies with normal development generally pay dividends every year. Shareholders receive dividends according to the proportion of equity they hold. The more equity held, the more dividends. Some companies also set up dual-class shares to obtain more or less dividends than the proportion of shares held.
- Salary: Generally, company partners also work full-time in the company. As company employees, they should receive labor remuneration.
- Bonus: Partners generally undertake special tasks, either R&D or business. To motivate everyone, the board of directors will set bonuses for specific tasks and provide appropriate incentives based on the degree of completion.

From the perspective of personal achievement:

The company is like the child of the partners. Everyone raises the company from scratch, from small to large. The whole process is full of challenges, and every progress makes everyone full of a sense of accomplishment. When the company grows into the company in everyone's mind, as a partner, there must be a full sense of accomplishment and satisfaction.

## Industry Trends

Xinjiang cotton came to the forefront due to the H&M incident. On April 3rd, the CCTV program 'Voice' specially invited Zhao Chunjiang, an academician in the field of smart agriculture, to support Xinjiang cotton. From the program, we can see that intelligence is the development trend of agriculture. Under the trend of unmanned and fewer people, agriculture will become more and more technological, and fewer and fewer people will be engaged in agriculture. Agriculture may become the first industry to achieve large-scale unmanned operations. In the future, people engaged in agriculture will mainly be artificial intelligence experts and biologists, and the cost elements of agricultural products will undergo fundamental changes. Energy costs will become the main component of agricultural products. Under the background of carbon grayity, silicon-based agriculture dominated by photovoltaics + technology will replace carbon-based agriculture. Companies and individuals engaged in agriculture must firmly grasp this trend.
