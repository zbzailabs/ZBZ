Today I attended an agricultural product exhibition with the theme of "Internet Celebrity Fresh Food Festival". Compared with the hot scene when the fifth edition was held a year ago, there were significantly fewer exhibitors and professional visitors.

The reason why it is deserted is, on the one hand, that the economy is in recession and the general environment is indeed not good.On the other hand, the majority of growers are anxious about the sales of the organizers and are tired of the hot-spot routine. In 2018, there will be new retail, and in 2025, Internet celebrities will bring goods. There is a theme every year, and the views are sharper and the behavior is outrageous every year.Showing off feelings cannot help new farmers save a little cost, improve a little quality, or bring a little sales.

A man who came from Inner Mongolia and sold melon seeds was handing out melon seeds to customers who were consulting in front of the platform. When he saw two live broadcast celebrities claimed by the organizer, he slowly came to the booth and said hello from a distance: "Little sister, please broadcast live for us."The little girl grabbed a handful of melon seeds and raised her phone while eating. The eldest brother hurriedly looked for the company banner. Here, the eldest brother was still looking for the banner. They had already walked away with the melon seeds. The eldest brother watched the two fat figures go away and muttered, Internet celebrities are so star-like.The customer next to me said, I just took a look at the internet celebrity's screen. There must be about two people watching it online, just like you melon seed live broadcast.Now take a mobile phone, open an account and say you are an internet celebrity.It's all about being high.

A young man from Sichuan complained to his companions in the corner that this exhibition was unprofessional. He should go back in the afternoon to repair the warehouse and prepare to collect oranges. You see, on the first day, an aunt came in with her grandson. It was so unprofessional.My companion spoke leisurely and authentically, which also brought some popularity.Otherwise, it’s just rainbow farts for each other when we grow fruit.

Your orange is not sweet. The audience took one bite and threw a piece of orange into the trash can. The exhibitor's brother smiled awkwardly, yes, yes, it will be on the market in 20 days. Now it has enough water, but the acidity is still a bit high, and the sugar is not enough.

Your grapefruit has no taste. The audience took one bite and threw a piece of grapefruit into the trash can. The exhibitor's sister said, "No, it will take two weeks before the grapefruit will be sweet. You can rest assured that it will be sweet."

Ah, you are a Xiangshan Red beauty, why aren’t you sweet?The audience took one bite and threw a piece of orange into the trash can. The exhibitor girl said that it will be ripe in 2 weeks, and it is too early now.

The timing of the exhibition was wrong. A large number of high-quality fruits were not in season yet, and neither exhibitors nor visitors could get a high-quality experience.This is also a trouble that non-professional exhibition organizers bring to exhibitors.

How many more times can such an exhibition be held?
