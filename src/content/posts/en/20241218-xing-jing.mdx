Recently, I watched "I am a Criminal Police Officer" starring Yu Hewei. I haven't watched domestic dramas for many years. Although domestic dramas have improved, they are not big. It seems that no one in the entire production team really cares about the quality of the drama. Old actors show off their acting skills for the sake of showing off, new actors can't read their lines completely, and post-production repairs the lines, and it turns out that they are not dubbed by the actors themselves. In addition, there is still much room for improvement in the screenwriter's ability. Whether it is books or film and television dramas, as long as different characters repeat the same sentence that the screenwriter thinks is important in a close space and timeline, it can be basically determined that the screenwriter's skills are not mature enough. For example, different characters in the play will repeat a sentence "Every case has the smell and temperament of every case".

Qin Chuan is a person who combines various advantages. The excellent qualities in him are the concentrated embodiment of the qualities of all those who can get things done. He combines all excellent qualities. To sum up, he has the following advantages. First, he wants to do things. In the Xishan coal mine explosion and robbery case, Qin Chuan's background and the reasons for his growth into a criminal police officer were explained clearly. He caught the main culprit of the police assault and gun robbery case. After the Xishan robbery case, the conflict between him and the criminal police captain Hu Bing further highlighted his personal willingness to do things. Furthermore, when he was transferred to the preliminary trial section, he not only took the initiative to work actively, but also led the old police officers to work together, bringing the preliminary trial work to a new height. Second, he is capable of doing things. Wanting to do things is subjective will, and being capable of doing things is personal ability. Only when you are capable of doing things and getting things done can there be further possibilities. Qin Chuan won the personal second-class merit in the detection of the Xishan coal mine explosion case, which is the organization's affirmation of his ability. Third, it is necessary to master the ways and methods of doing things. When Qin Chuan became President Qin, especially in the process of guiding various cases, he provided a set of feasible ways and methods for the organizers. For example, in the detection of the Wang Li infringement of three minor children case, Team Tao played by Fu Dalong formed a sharp contrast with Qin Chuan. In the detection of the two Qingjiang cases, Yang Xiong played by Zhu Hui also formed a sharp contrast with Qin Chuan. Apart from their common qualities as criminal police, what Qin Chuan has more than the other two is the ways and methods of doing things. After Qin Chuan took over the two cases, the first thing he did was to define the nature of the case, whether it was for money or murder, and whether it was a local or an outsider. After determining the core problem first, and then conducting detection around the core problem at all times. This is very important. Once the direction of handling the case is wrong, there will inevitably be no correct detection result. That is to say, it is necessary to make a good start. Fourth, the ability to mobilize resources. The more new technologies are applied, the more it tests the ability of resource scheduling. Qin Chuan started with only two soldiers and formed a four-person group with his friends. Later, he became the captain of a provincial criminal investigation corps. His ability to schedule resources is completely different. In the Zhang Kehan case, he hoped to have 200 people screen the video but could only mobilize 80 people. By the time he solved the backlog cases, he could already mobilize the province's resources for DNA testing.

From the play, we can learn some knowledge about crime. First, homicide crimes generally include two types: murder for money and murder for life. Among them, murder for money usually involves killing, but pure revenge killing rarely involves property infringement. Second, the current detection rate of homicide cases has reached more than 90%, even before the on-site investigation is completed, and suspects can be locked through technical means. Third, the main work of criminal investigation is to find suspects based on traces. The more no traces can be found at the scene, the more it shows that the criminal came prepared. Fourth, cases that are difficult to solve are not necessarily committed by smart suspects, but may also be committed by an inconspicuous person on a whim.
