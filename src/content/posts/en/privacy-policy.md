**Effective Date: September 25, 2024**

Welcome to [DiMi](https://zbz.ai). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our website and services.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you visit our website or interact with us, we may collect the following types of information:

- **Personally Identifiable Information**: Such as name, email address, etc., especially when you use the comment system on our website.
- **Non-Personally Identifiable Information**: Such as browser type, operating system, referring URLs, your activity on our website, etc.
- **Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies**: We use Cookies and other technologies to collect information, enhance user experience, and analyze website traffic.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

- To provide, operate, and maintain our website.
- To analyze user behavior and optimize website experience.
- To personalize advertising content and show you relevant ads.
- To process user comments and interactions.
- To comply with legal obligations and ensure data security.

## 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and store your information. Below are the types of Cookies we use and their purposes:

- **Essential Cookies**: These Cookies ensure the basic functions of our website operate properly.
- **Analytics Cookies**: We use analytics tools like Google Analytics and Umami to collect and analyze user behavior data to improve our website services. These tools may use Cookies or similar technologies to track user activity on our website.

  **[Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/analytics/web/)**: Google Analytics uses Cookies to collect information about how users use our website. You can choose to opt-out of these statistics by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

  **[Umami](https://umami.is/)**: Umami is an open-source analytics tool we use to anonymously track user behavior. It does not collect personally identifiable information.

- **Advertising Cookies**: We use Google AdSense to provide personalized advertising services. Google AdSense may use Cookies to track user browsing behavior on our website and across the internet, and display personalized ads based on this information.

  **[Google AdSense](https://www.google.com/adsense/start/)**: As a third-party advertising vendor, Google may use Cookies and other technologies to collect information about your use of our website. Google uses this information to serve targeted ads. For more information, please refer to the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You can manage Cookie preferences through your browser settings, choosing to delete or disable some or all Cookies. However, disabling Cookies may affect your experience using our website.

## 4. Comment System

We use **[Waline](https://waline.js.org/)** as the website's comment system, allowing users to post comments on our website. The information you submit when commenting (such as username, email address, and comment content) may be stored on our servers. Please note that Waline is a backend-less comment system, your comment data will be stored on our website servers and will not be shared with third parties.

## 5. Use of Third-Party Services

We may share your information with the following third parties for purposes including website analysis, ad serving, etc.:

- **Google Analytics**: We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze user data to help us understand how users interact with our website. The data collected by Google Analytics is generally anonymous.
- **Google AdSense**: Google AdSense is the advertising service provider we use, providing personalized ads through its advertising system.
- **Umami**: We use Umami as an analytics tool to help us track user behavior and improve the website.

These third parties may collect user data and use this information in accordance with their privacy policies. Please refer to each service provider's privacy policy for more details.

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

- Sharing with service providers to enable them to provide support services.
- As required by law, or to protect our legal rights and property.

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no method of online data transmission can guarantee 100% security.

## 8. Data Storage and International Transfer

Our servers are located in Hong Kong, China. Regardless of which country you reside in, your visiting our website and submitting information signifies your consent that your personal information may be transferred to and stored in Hong Kong, China, or other places where data protection laws may differ from your country. We will take appropriate measures to ensure your data is protected during cross-border transmission.

## 9. User Privacy Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR and CCPA), you have the following rights:

- Access your personal information we collect.
- Request correction of your information.
- Request deletion of your information (in certain cases).
- Refuse our direct marketing of your personal data.
- Withdraw your consent and stop our processing of your personal information.

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page, and updates will be effective from the date of posting. We recommend you review this policy regularly to understand the latest privacy practices.
