### Weekly Reflections

The last day of 2020 was spent in peace and tranquility. Thinking back to the beginning of the year when the epidemic was raging, everything was full of uncertainty. Under panic, I cleared my positions at the lowest point. Then I researched the development laws of the 2003 epidemic, and after comparing it with this epidemic, [I came to the conclusion that there is no need to panic](https:// .xyz/covid-19/), and slowly increased my positions again, operating with full positions. When the domestic epidemic ended, and I thought this catastrophe had passed, who knew that all capitalism is a paper tiger. The epidemic broke out overseas, and it was wave after wave, with no end in sight. Stepping into 2021, the overseas epidemic is still constantly breaking records. On January 1, 2021, the daily number of new deaths from the new crown overseas was still breaking records, with more than 18,000 deaths in a single day. The domestic situation is not completely peaceful either, with more than a thousand confirmed cases on record daily, and there are still sporadic victims every day. Now the treatment experience and level are far beyond the same period last year, yet people are still dying, which shows the harm of the new crown virus.

After experiencing the epidemic, the wave of corporate bankruptcies, floods, and the Sino-US trade war, people have become calmer and calmer, and everyone believes that nothing worse will happen. However, black swans and gray rhinos always exist, maybe they will never appear, maybe they will appear in the next moment, so it is better to be cautious in everything.

No harm without comparison, an epidemic punctured all the paper tigers of the West, a trade war made people see the cruelty of competition between countries. Fortunately, we stood on the right side. Specifically in the stock market, the ChiNext has topped the world for three consecutive years. In an automatically ascending elevator like China, even those like me who sold at a loss at a low level, presumably made a profit throughout the year. [In dialectical learning, we summarized the investment insights of masters. Now it seems that winning the ovarian lottery is already an established fact. As for betting on the country's fortune, it can be summarized by a sentence that is most deeply rooted in people's hearts, spoken by Luo Zhenyu, a pseudo-Buffett quote: No one can succeed by shorting their own motherland. The closest to this pseudo-Buffett value-style quote is another factual rhetorical question from Buffett: who has ever benefited during the past 238 years by betting against America? Simply put, going long on China is betting right on the country's fortune. And to compete with people in the same elevator, the only thing to do is to be like Buffett "When investing, we view ourselves as business analysts - not as market analysts, not as macroeconomic analysts, and not even as security analysts", only in this way can we discover one wet and slippery snow track after another, dare to concentrate holdings, and place heavy bets.

### Weekly Events

On December 30, 2020, the leaders of China and the EU jointly announced the completion of the China-EU investment agreement negotiations as scheduled. The China-EU Investment Agreement is another high-level cross-regional economic agreement welcomed by China following the signing of the Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership (RCEP) on November 15, 2020. In 2020, China achieved fruitful results in expanding its "circle of friends". Following the three major free trade zones, another influential free trade agreement was completed.

Currently, there are three major free trade zones in the world:

- North American Free Trade Area (NAFTA): Established by the United States, Canada, and Mexico on August 12, 1992.

- European Union (EU): Established by Germany, France, Italy, the Netherlands, Belgium, and Luxembourg on November 1, 1993, and now has 27 member states.

- Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership (RCEP): Initiated by the ten member states of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN), with the participation of China, Japan, South Korea, Australia, and New Zealand, five countries that have free trade agreements with ASEAN, and established on November 15, 2020.

- China-EU Bilateral Investment Agreement (BIT): Signed by China and the EU, aiming to build an institutional arrangement for China-EU bilateral investment. The core content of the agreement includes four aspects (1) ensuring mutual investment protection, respecting intellectual property rights, and ensuring subsidy transparency; (2) improving market access conditions for both sides; (3) ensuring a clear, fair, and transparent investment environment and regulatory procedures; (4) improving labor standards and supporting sustainable development.
