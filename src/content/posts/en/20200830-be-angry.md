### Weekly Thoughts

ByteDance sued the US government. Although the chances of winning are slim, it showed an attitude. People live for a breath, and Buddha fights for a stick of incense. When it is time to fight, you must fight, even if your head is broken and bleeding, you must let the other party know that you are not to be trifled with.

In life, we often encounter unreasonable people. If you take a step back, it will not broaden the horizon, but make the other party take a step forward. Therefore, when encountering unreasonable people, you must resolutely fight back, even if you lose, you must make the other party pay the price.
