### Weekly Thoughts

Jinglin Assets is the only local private equity fund with a scale of over 100 billion. Its fund management partner, Mr. Gao Yuncheng, said in a speech: Jinglin Assets' core strategy is: fundamental research determines value, reverse investment determines price. Specifically, through boring, SOP-like fundamental framework analysis to study the quality of the target, after selecting potential investment targets, when the stock price reaches a reasonable price, then buy heavily. Due to the scarcity of targets and the openness of information, the market does not leave many opportunities for investors to buy at a reasonable price. At this time, it is necessary to use reverse investment thinking and buy when the stock price plunges.

Using the most universal concepts, rational cognition and determined action, Jinglin Assets can achieve the number one position.
