### Weekly Reflections

In the article "Finding Technology for the Industry", it was predicted that the theme of the 2021 No. 1 document would be "Opinions on Comprehensively Promoting Rural Revitalization and Accelerating the Modernization of Agriculture and Rural Areas", and might focus on three aspects. Today, Xinhua News Agency authorized the release of the [full text of the document](http://www.xinhuanet.com/politics/2021-02/21/c_1127122068.htm), and all predictions were verified.

Looking at the full text, there are some key points worth noting.

- **General Requirements**
  - Insist on the integrated design of agricultural modernization and rural modernization (Separation of agriculture and rural areas, a new formulation).
  - Fully utilize functions such as agricultural product supply, ecological barriers, and cultural inheritance (Functions of rural areas).
  - Grain output must reach more than 1.3 trillion jin (650 million tons). This is the first time a specific figure has been set for the grain output target, and the non-grain use of arable land must be resolutely curbed. The pig industry needs to develop smoothly (Due to the production cycle, the law of agricultural product supply has become a roller coaster industry. In the future, appropriate adjustments will be made to ensure the stability of the number and scale of enterprises, stable pork supply, and prevent pork prices from skyrocketing and plummeting).
  - Launch agricultural and rural modernization planning (What is agricultural and rural modernization with Chinese characteristics? Focus during the 14th Five-Year Plan. By 2025, significant progress will be made in agricultural and rural modernization (Will areas with favorable conditions basically achieve agricultural modernization? What is agricultural modernization?), agricultural infrastructure modernization will reach a new level, rural living facilities facilitation will be preliminarily realized, and the level of equalization of basic public services in urban and rural areas will be significantly improved).

- **Achieve Effective Connection between Consolidating and Expanding Poverty Alleviation Achievements and Rural Revitalization**
  - A 5-year transition period, to help send them off. The next step after poverty alleviation is to achieve a moderately prosperous society and rural revitalization, and rural revitalization funds will be transferred to poverty-stricken counties.
  - Implement and promote "relief-for-work" in the field of agricultural infrastructure, just like during the urbanization process, local infrastructure companies will benefit.

- **Accelerate Agricultural Modernization**
  - Shared responsibility of the Party and government for food security, which was originally the governor's responsibility system. Improve the governor's responsibility system for food security and the mayor's responsibility system for the "vegetable basket". All provinces (autonomous regions and municipalities directly under the central government) must stabilize the grain sowing area and increase the yield per unit. Previously, requirements were looser for economically developed areas. Now, districts, counties, and cities that still have agricultural arable land in economically developed areas will become the main force of agricultural production.
  - Establish a national food security industrial belt to ensure that grain farmers have reasonable returns.
  - Four major crops to watch: rice, wheat, corn, soybeans, and rapeseed and peanuts whose areas will explode. Will the southern rice-growing areas implement rice-wheat rotation or rice-oil rotation in the future?
  - Pilot full cost insurance and income insurance for the three major staple grains: rice, wheat, and corn (Who will benefit?).
  - Deepen the high-quality grain project: What project is this?
  - Protect the basic production capacity of pigs: Will it be in the form of subsidies and national inventory regulation? Which companies will be included?
  - Pork substitutes: Cattle and sheep industry.
  - Fight a good battle for the seed industry turnaround.
    - Establish crop, livestock and poultry, and marine fishery germplasm resource banks.
    - Long-term support for basic research in breeding and key breeding, universities and research institutes will not lack scientific research funding.
    - Emphasize respecting science and recommending biological breeding, anti-GMO diehards can rest.
    - Support leading seed industry companies to establish commercial breeding.
    - Study post-subsidy policies for the R&D and promotion of major varieties.
  - Stick to the 1.8 billion mu red line: The terms used in this paragraph are the strictest, having teeth, resolutely curbing, etc. The opportunity lies in the construction of new high-standard farmland.
  - Insist on self-reliance in agricultural technology and green agricultural development: Intelligent equipment and domestic substitution of green agricultural materials have become the trend and mainstream, foreign brands are no longer effective.

- **Vigorously Implement Rural Construction Actions**
  - Implement the digital countryside construction and development project. Promote the simultaneous planning and construction of rural gigabit optical networks, fifth-generation mobile communications (5G), and mobile Internet of Things with cities. Accelerate the construction of space-based facilities such as agricultural and rural remote sensing satellites. Develop smart agriculture, establish an agricultural and rural big data system, and promote the deep integration of new generation information technology with agricultural production and operation. Improve the agricultural meteorological comprehensive monitoring network, and enhance the ability to prevent agricultural meteorological disasters.
  - Carry out reading and farming education.
  - Accelerate the implementation of the agricultural product cold chain logistics facility construction project, and promote field small-scale warehousing and preservation cold chain facilities.
  - Promote urbanization with the county seat as an important carrier, and build the county seat according to the standards of small cities in areas with favorable conditions.
  - Support cities and counties to build a shared agriculture-related credit information database within the domain, and basically build a relatively complete new agricultural operation entity credit system within 3 years. Develop rural digital inclusive finance. Vigorously carry out small credit loans for farmer households, insurance policy pledge loans, and agricultural machinery and greenhouse facility mortgage loans. Encourage the development of exclusive financial products to support new agricultural operation entities and rural new industries and new formats, and increase first loans and credit loans.

- **Strengthen the Party's Leadership over Rural Work**
  - Actively promote village party organization secretaries to serve as directors of village committees through legal procedures in places with favorable conditions.
  - Strengthen emergency management and fire safety system construction in counties, townships, and villages, and do a good job in risk assessment, monitoring and early warning, and emergency disposal of major incidents such as natural disasters, public health, and safety hazards.
