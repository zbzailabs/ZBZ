**Thoughts**

The consecutive limit-up strategy is a unique trading strategy in the A-share market. It utilizes the price limit system and, through capital advantage and emotional gaming, obtains huge profits in the short term. The core of this strategy is "momentum", that is, following the trend, buying high and selling higher.

This strategy requires extremely strict discipline and execution ability, as well as a keen insight into market sentiment. For most retail investors, this is a very dangerous game, and it is easy to become the "leeks" being harvested.
