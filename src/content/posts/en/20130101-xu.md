In ancient times, people recorded life using text on stones, turtle shells, and bronze vessels.

Recording was a privilege of the powerful.

Today, we have a faster way to store our thoughts: the internet.

We also have a faster way to share our perspectives: the internet.

Recording one’s life through words, for an individual, is eternity.

A faithful record of life and living it earnestly.
