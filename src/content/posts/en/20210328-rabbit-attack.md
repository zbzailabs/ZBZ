## Weekly Reflections

After a new round of verbal abuse, the Rabbit immediately contacted the Big Bear and the Camel, swearing an ironclad alliance relationship. The North Stick also test-fired two intercontinental missiles to show support. At the same time, the Rabbit also imposed sanctions on individual representatives of the Bald Eagle's allies, such as the Bull, the Tiger, and the Beaver. The Rabbit Country's Wolf Warrior troops and netizens also launched a counterattack against a statement issued by the Bald Eagle Alliance in September last year boycotting cotton from the Lamb Skewer region.

When the rabbit starts biting, the Bald Eagle and its allies, who have dominated the jungle for many years, will inevitably not let it go. The rabbit must be prepared for evolution or even species mutation, developing into a more combat-capable species to engage in a more brutal struggle.

This is inevitably a life-and-death struggle. Either the East Wind prevails over the West Wind, or the West Wind prevails over the East Wind. Only by achieving an overwhelming victory in the great changes unseen in a century can the foundation be laid for the next centenary goal.
