## Meme Coin

Meme Coin is a cryptocurrency based on Internet memes or pop culture, usually with humor or community participation as the main attraction. According to Wikipedia: Meme coin, Meme Coin may originate from specific memes (such as DogeCoin) or celebrities (such as Coinye), and is sometimes considered a "shitcoin" due to its lack of value, authenticity, or utility. However, some Meme Coins such as Dogecoin, after launching in 2013, gained high market capitalization and social influence through social media spread.

Meme Coins: What They Are, Examples, Pros and Cons points out that Meme Coins usually rely on humor and community commitment to attract users, examples include Dogecoin and Shiba Inu. ## Trading Strategies

### Strategy Goals

- Return Goal: Double capital (100% return) or higher through multiple volatility cycles.
- Risk Tolerance: Control single maximum loss within 20% of initial capital.
- Applicable Scenarios: Short-term high volatility Meme coins (such as Bonk, Dogwifhat), trading in the Solana ecosystem.

### Trading Rules

1. Buying and Selling Rules

- Buying Point: After the initial buy, every time the price drops to 50% below the previous selling point, use all cash obtained from the last sale to buy.
- Selling Point: When the price rises by 100%, sell 30% of the holding (instead of 50%), to lock in some profits and retain more room for upside.
- Adjustment Reason: Selling 30% reduces the risk of missing larger gains, while maintaining sufficient holdings to deal with subsequent fluctuations.

1. Stop Loss and Take Profit

- Stop Loss: If the price drops to 50% of the initial buying price (or a lower custom point), clear the position and exit to avoid large losses.
- Take Profit: If the price rises to 300% of the previous selling point (i.e., 3 times), sell 50% of the holding to lock in larger profits.
- Adjustment Reason: Increase stop loss to protect principal, and take profit to capture unexpected large gains.

1. Trading Frequency and Money Management

- Initial Capital: Invest an amount you can afford to lose (e.g., $100).
- Single Trade: Use only current holdings and cash for each buy and sell, do not add additional funds.
- Number of Cycles: Target 5-10 volatility cycles, or adjust according to market conditions.

1. Coin Selection

- Criteria: Prioritize high liquidity, active community Meme coins on Solana (such as Bonk, Dogwifhat, Fartcoin), avoid small market cap or newly issued low liquidity coins.
- Tools: Check market cap and trading volume via CoinGecko Solana Meme Coins.
