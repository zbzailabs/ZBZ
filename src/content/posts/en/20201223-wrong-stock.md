A ghost in the dark night sky asks a soul-searching question: what is the biggest mistake you have made in your life?

The answer from someone as cruel as Lao Rongzhi is, apart from trading stocks, I have never done anything wrong.

The answer from someone as rich as Jack Ma is, the biggest mistake in my life was founding Alibaba.

The answer from someone as powerful as Trump is, the day I won the election, I collapsed, because I no longer had my original life, my previous life was awesome.

Can you believe the words of a murderer?
