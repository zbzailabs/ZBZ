Rongcheng is a county-level city under the jurisdiction of Weihai City, Shandong Province. Located at the easternmost tip of the Shandong Peninsula, it is surrounded by the sea on three sides: north, east, and south.

Rongcheng has a beautiful environment and a pleasant climate. The annual average temperature is around 12 degrees Celsius, the air is fresh, and the water quality is excellent. It is a city very suitable for living and retirement, known as the "Hometown of Longevity".

Rongcheng is also famous for its abundant seafood. Due to its unique geographical location, the seafood here is fresh, delicious, and diverse. Sea cucumbers, abalone, scallops, shrimp, and crabs are all local specialties.

In addition, Rongcheng has rich tourism resources. Swan Lake is the largest wintering habitat for swans in Asia, with tens of thousands of swans migrating here every winter. Chengshantou, known as "China's Cape of Good Hope", is the place where Emperor Qin Shi Huang first saw the sunrise.
