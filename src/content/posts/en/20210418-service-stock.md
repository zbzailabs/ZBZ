### Weekly Reflections

"Female Stock God" Cathie Wood stated that she will avoid financial and energy stocks, as they will be the most disrupted and disintermediated sectors in the next 5 years. Wood pointed out that since contactless payments have become quite important, financial stocks such as banks may be hurt. The S&P 500 Financial and Energy Indices have risen about 19% and 29% respectively so far this year. Instead, Wood focuses on "innovation platforms", such as DNA sequencing, robotics, energy storage and automobiles, artificial intelligence, and blockchain technology. She said: "Nothing can stop them now."

## Weekly Events

The China Securities Regulatory Commission clarified the attributes of science and technology innovation. Fintech and model innovation enterprises are restricted from listing on the Science and Technology Innovation Board, aiming to "cultivate more innovative enterprises with hard technology strength and market competitiveness". This is a primary standard for testing whether the Science and Technology Innovation Board is successful.
