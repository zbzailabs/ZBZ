The Fourth Plenary Session of the 20th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China was held from October 20-23, 2025. It reviewed and adopted the "Proposals of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Formulating the Fifteenth Five-Year Plan for National Economic and Social Development", basically setting the tone for China's development in the next 5-10 years.

## Historical Positioning of the 15th Five-Year Plan

The "15th Five-Year Plan" period is a key period for **consolidating foundations and exerting full force**. Formulating and implementing the "15th Five-Year Plan" well will lay a more solid foundation for basically achieving socialist modernization by 2035. The 15th Five-Year Plan holds an important position of connecting the past and the future in the process of basically achieving socialist modernization. We must deeply understand the decisive significance of the "Two Establishments", which means that the core leadership will remain stable during the 15th Five-Year Plan period.

## Overall Judgment of China's Development Environment during the 15th Five-Year Plan

Relations between major powers affect the international situation, and the evolution of the international situation profoundly affects domestic development. China's development is in a period where strategic opportunities and risks and challenges coexist, and uncertain and unpredictable factors are increasing. From an international perspective, the evolution of changes unseen in a century is accelerating, and the balance of international power is undergoing profound adjustments. From a domestic perspective, China's economic foundation is stable, with many advantages, strong resilience, and great potential. The supporting conditions and basic trend of long-term improvement have not changed. "During the '15th Five-Year Plan' period, we must not only take advantage of the momentum but also face difficulties head-on, transforming advantages in all aspects into actual effectiveness of high-quality development."

## Main Objectives of the 15th Five-Year Plan

By 2030, the objectives of the 15th Five-Year Plan will be completed, significant results will be achieved in high-quality development, the level of scientific and technological self-reliance and self-strengthening will be greatly improved, new breakthroughs will be made in further comprehensively deepening reforms, the degree of social civilization will be significantly improved, people's quality of life will continue to improve, new major progress will be made in the construction of a Beautiful China, and the national security barrier will be further consolidated.

- During the "15th Five-Year Plan" period, the centenary goal of the military must be achieved as scheduled.
- By the 80th anniversary of the founding of New China, the reform tasks proposed by the Third Plenary Session of the 20th Central Committee of the Party will be completed.
- By 2035, reach the level of moderately developed countries and basically achieve socialist modernization.

**Vision 2035 Objectives**
Looking ahead to 2035, China will basically achieve socialist modernization. Economic strength, scientific and technological strength, and comprehensive national power will leap significantly. The total economic volume and per capita income of urban and rural residents will step up to a new large level. Key core technologies will achieve major breakthroughs, entering the forefront of innovative countries. Basically achieve new industrialization, informatization, urbanization, and agricultural modernization, and build a modern economic system. Basically achieve the modernization of the national governance system and governance capabilities, fully guarantee people's rights to equal participation and equal development, and basically build a country ruled by law, a government ruled by law, and a society ruled by law. Build a strong cultural country, a strong educational country, a strong talent country, a strong sports country, and a Healthy China. The quality of nationals and the degree of social civilization will reach a new height, and the national cultural soft power will be significantly enhanced. Green production and lifestyle will be widely formed, carbon emissions will stabilize and then decline after peaking, the ecological environment will fundamentally improve, and the goal of building a Beautiful China will be basically achieved. A new pattern of opening up to the outside world will be formed, and new advantages in participating in international economic cooperation and competition will be significantly enhanced. Per capita GDP will reach the level of moderately developed countries, the middle-income group will expand significantly, basic public services will be equalized, and the development gap between urban and rural areas and regions and the gap in residents' living standards will be significantly narrowed. The construction of a Peaceful China will reach a higher level, and the modernization of national defense and the military will be basically achieved. People's lives will be better, and more obvious substantive progress will be made in the comprehensive development of people and the common prosperity of all people.

To basically achieve socialist modernization by 2035, it needs to be completed through the implementation of the three five-year plans: the "14th Five-Year Plan", "15th Five-Year Plan", and "16th Five-Year Plan".

**Build a Modern Industrial System, Consolidate and Strengthen the Foundation of the Real Economy**
China's economy started with the real economy and must also rely on the real economy to move towards the future.
Insist on placing the focus of economic development on the real economy[^1], adhere to the direction of intelligence, greening, and integration, accelerate the construction of a manufacturing power, a quality power, an aerospace power, a transportation power, and a network power, maintain a reasonable proportion of manufacturing, and build a modern industrial system with advanced manufacturing as the backbone. It is necessary to optimize and upgrade traditional industries (preliminary estimates suggest that there will be a new market space of about 10 trillion yuan in the next 5 years), cultivate and expand emerging industries and future industries (the new scale in the next 10 years is equivalent to rebuilding a Chinese high-tech industry), promote the high-quality and efficient development of the service industry, and build a modern infrastructure system.

[^1] Real Economy: The real economy refers to ==all economic activities such as the production and circulation of material and spiritual products, including agriculture, industry, service industry, education, culture and other fields, which is the basis for the survival and development of human society==. It is opposite to the virtual economy. The virtual economy (such as financial speculation) is virtual capital activity based on the real economy, while participants in the real economy focus on economic activities that meet real needs.
