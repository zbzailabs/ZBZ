## 1. Consolidate and Expand the Achievements of Poverty Alleviation, Comprehensively Promote Rural Revitalization

### (1) The Importance and Arduousness of the Task of Consolidating Poverty Alleviation Achievements

Eliminating poverty and achieving common prosperity is the essential requirement of socialism and the original mission of the Party. Since the reform and opening up, the government has always regarded solving the poverty problem as the primary task. Innovative measures such as development-oriented poverty alleviation driven by rural reform, development-style poverty alleviation, and targeted poverty alleviation have been carried out, completing poverty alleviation tasks in different periods and basically eliminating absolute poverty. Consolidating the achievements of poverty alleviation, preventing returning to poverty and new poor population, and holding the bottom line of absolute poverty are the basic prerequisites for achieving common prosperity. The principal contradiction in our country at present and for a period of time in the future is the contradiction between the people's ever-growing needs for a better life and unbalanced and inadequate development. Therefore, for a period of time in the future, the problems of unbalanced regional development, unbalanced urban and rural development, and inadequate rural development will still exist, which determines that the problem of relative poverty will exist for a long time, and there are still relatively large risks of returning to poverty in some areas.

### (2) The Task of Consolidating Poverty Alleviation Achievements Promotes Comprehensive Rural Revitalization

1. Consolidate poverty alleviation achievements and promote rural industrial revitalization.
2. Consolidate poverty alleviation achievements and promote rural ecological revitalization.
3. Consolidate poverty alleviation achievements and promote rural talent revitalization.
4. Consolidate poverty alleviation achievements and promote rural cultural revitalization.
5. Consolidate poverty alleviation achievements and promote rural organizational revitalization.

## 2. Accelerate the Promotion of Agricultural and Rural Modernization

### (1) Guiding Ideology

Adhere to the priority development of agriculture and rural areas, adhere to the integrated design and joint promotion of agricultural modernization and rural modernization, with promoting high-quality development as the theme, ensuring supply security and revitalizing circulation as the work positioning, deepening the agricultural supply-side structural reform, placing rural construction in an important position in socialist modernization, achieving effective connection between consolidating and expanding poverty alleviation achievements and rural revitalization, comprehensively promoting the revitalization of rural industries, talents, culture, ecology, and organizations, accelerating the formation of a new type of industry-agriculture-urban-rural relationship featuring mutual promotion of industry and agriculture, urban-rural complementarity, coordinated development, and common prosperity, promoting high-quality and efficient agriculture, livable and business-friendly rural areas, and affluent farmers, and providing strong support for comprehensively building a modern socialist country.

### (2) Main Objectives

By 2025, the agricultural foundation will be more solid, the rural revitalization strategy will be comprehensively promoted, and important progress will be made in agricultural and rural modernization. Promote areas with favorable conditions to take the lead in basically achieving agricultural and rural modernization, and achieve effective connection between consolidating poverty alleviation achievements and rural revitalization in poverty-stricken areas.

1. Effective guarantee of the supply of grain and other important agricultural products.
2. Steady improvement in agricultural quality, efficiency, and competitiveness.
3. New progress in rural infrastructure construction.
4. Significant improvement in the rural ecological environment.
5. Further enhancement of rural governance capabilities.
6. Steady growth in rural residents' income.
7. Consolidation and expansion of poverty alleviation achievements.

Looking forward to 2035, decisive progress will be made in comprehensive rural revitalization, and agricultural and rural modernization will be basically achieved.
