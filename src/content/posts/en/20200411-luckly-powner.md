I often hear people say that money earned by luck is lost by ability. This is not a poisonous chicken soup, but a proverb. Ability is strong or weak. People with weak ability, even if a pie falls into their hands, cannot hold it, because the free-falling pie is too fast, and the arm strength is insufficient. Not only can they not catch the pie, but if they catch it recklessly, they may hurt their hands.
