1. Clarify the company's needs. Obtaining government project funding must be based on the company's development needs, finding the most matching resources.
2. Each region has clear assessment policies. It is necessary to understand policy redemption, performance assessment, and company registration location requirements, and determine whether assessment indicators can be met based on the company's strength.
3. Find a reliable intermediary to save time and avoid the trouble of directly docking with government investment promotion departments one by one.
