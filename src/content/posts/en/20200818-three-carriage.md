### Weekly Thoughts

Economic development needs three carriages to drive it. Last week, we mentioned that new infrastructure is one of the carriages under the new normal. The other two carriages are new consumption and new investment.

The contradiction between production relations and productive forces, and the contradiction between the superstructure and the economic base have transformed into the contradiction between the people's growing needs for a better life and unbalanced and inadequate development. In the future, consumption that meets new life needs will be the main driving force for economic development. Which consumer industries have huge development potential in the future? This can be calculated by dividing their industrial indicators by the population base of 1.4 billion. For example, in 2020, the number of cars in the country was 260 million, with an average of 0.18 cars per person. Similar industries include various consumer electronics, medical resources, education resources, etc.

In the past, when we talked about investment, we mainly referred to infrastructure investment and project investment. With the improvement of the rule of law and the storing of wealth among the people, residents' demand for asset diversification will also drive the development of banks, brokerages, insurance, and trusts.

Institutional investors have become the main force in the market. This round of bull market will not be a comprehensive bull market, but a structural bull market, and new infrastructure, new consumption, and new investment will be the main sectors for the structural bull market rise. Specifically for individual stocks, it is necessary to select stocks with "Davis Double Play" characteristics.
