**Events**

The novel coronavirus pneumonia has spread to the whole world. According to JP Morgan's statistics, the world epidemic situation is still complicated. JP Morgan divides countries with more than 250 confirmed cases into four stages:

1. Early development: Countries are reporting new infections, but the overall total number of infections is relatively small. The Netherlands (265) / UK (273) fall into this category.

2. Acceleration: The growth rate of daily new infections is on an upward trend, and the overall scale of infections is relatively large. The number of infections is growing rapidly, but the recovery rate is very limited. The United States (537) / Japan (502) / Spain (673) / Germany (1040) / France (1126) fall into this category.

3. Late accumulation: The growth rate of daily new infections declines. But daily new cases are still considerable, but the number of recoveries has a higher growth rate. South Korea (7382) / Italy (7375) / Iran (6566) fall into this category.

4. Recovery: Daily reported new cases are limited. The increase in the number of recoveries is greater. China (80735 people) is currently in the recovery stage.
