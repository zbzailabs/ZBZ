### Weekly Reflections

Following the trend and speculating on hot spots will lose sooner or later, and the loss will be very fast. This time, following the trend and speculating on the "carbon grayity" concept Feida Environmental Protection, from floating profit to floating loss, and finally cutting the meat and leaving. Originally, I hadn't followed the trend and speculated on concepts for a long time, but analyzing the "carbon grayity" concept is a rare hot theme in the recent market, so I followed the trend and speculated, and also vowed that this is an epic concept comparable to the "millennium plan" Xiong'an New Area, but forgot that the concept stocks of the millennium plan back then were also a mess in the end. In fact, I didn't forget it at all. When I bought Feida Environmental Protection this time, I also thought that the concept stocks would collapse, but I still bought it with a gambler's mentality, and finally lost money. It was a bloody lesson. What about the value investment mentioned?

And recently, there is a thing going viral in the social circle today. An aunt in Dalian bought 50,000 yuan of Changchun High-tech in 2008, and then forgot about it. Recently, she went to cancel her account and found that there were more than 5 million yuan. The old lady was stimulated and couldn't stand steadily. Fortunately, the securities company employees supported her. The stock price of Changchun High-tech has risen by more than 10,000% from 2008 to now, and it is indeed true that 50,000 has become 5 million.

Facts have proved that as long as you buy a good company and hold it for a long time, don't chase the rise and fall every day, and don't speculate, A-shares will indeed give you a surprise.
