Since 2023, ChatGPT has evolved from a simple conversation generator model into an indispensable tool in our daily work and life. Its powerful functions and adaptability allow us to complete many tasks with it, from writing scripts to reviewing contracts, and even helping us answer various questions in life. Here are some experiences on how to use ChatGPT in daily work.

**Writing Scripts**

Although ChatGPT's programming capabilities are not perfect and sometimes lead us astray, it can help us complete simple scripting tasks. For example, batch renaming files, generating Markdown metadata, or even generating Ethereum vanity addresses or executing GitHub actions. As long as clear instructions are given, ChatGPT can produce scripts that meet the requirements.

**Assisting with Article Writing**

We can also use ChatGPT to help us write articles. It can imitate the tone of great writers like Lu Xun or Hu Shi and generate articles that suit our needs. This allows us to focus more on content creation rather than getting stuck on writing style and language.

**Reviewing Contracts**

ChatGPT can also assist us in reviewing contracts. It can quickly spot unfavorable clauses for the second party and summarize valuable insights. This way, we can focus more on the main content of the contract without worrying about potential issues.

**Writing Video Scripts**

ChatGPT can even help us create video promotional materials. Simply provide the manuscript to ChatGPT, have it rewritten as a video script, make slight refinements, and a high-quality video can be generated. This allows us to complete product promotions more quickly and improve work efficiency.

**Answering Queries**

Another powerful feature of ChatGPT is its ability to answer queries. We can submit various questions to ChatGPT, and it will provide tailored responses. This is because ChatGPT is a generative AI capable of providing customized answers based on questions, something traditional search engines cannot achieve.

**Tips for Using ChatGPT**

There are two tips that can help us make better use of ChatGPT. First, we can break tasks down into smaller questions and let ChatGPT complete the tasks step by step. Second, we should ask questions with emotion, treating ChatGPT as if it were a person sitting face-to-face. It’s best to have it play a specific role based on the question. This way, we can better leverage its generative capabilities and obtain higher-quality answers.

Overall, ChatGPT has become a new tool in our daily work and life. With proper use and techniques, we can gain significant help from it, improving our work efficiency and quality of life.
