**Thoughts**

People often argue about whether value investing is better or speculation. In fact, there is no distinction between high and low in investment, only making money and losing money. Whether it is value investing or speculation, as long as it makes money, it is smart investing.

Value investing is buying good things cheap and holding them for a long time; speculation is taking advantage of market fluctuations to make a quick profit. Both require different skills and knowledge. The key is to find the method that suits you and stick to it.
