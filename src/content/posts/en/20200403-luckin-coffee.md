**Thoughts**

Reread Peter Thiel's "Zero to One" this week. This small book published in 2015, as a lecture note for a Stanford University entrepreneurship course, does not have much hard work, but several points mentioned in it, such as PayPal, Tesla, digital currency, and how to avoid competitive industries, build monopoly enterprises, focus on marketing, and select employees and partners, are still worth thinking about.
