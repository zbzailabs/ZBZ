## Everyone Should Be Positive

When the epidemic was relaxed last week, some people looked forward to restoring their past lives, some were excited to resume trips that had not been made for a long time, and some took the initiative to seek positive results in order to integrate into life faster. However, with its rapid spread and varied infection symptoms, Omicron quickly extinguished all illusions. Masks and antigen test cards were sold out, and the medical material production and supply system was vulnerable under the fierce attack of the virus. Patients in many places were forced to resist the virus with their bodies.

When almost all infected people around us developed various unbearable symptoms, Xinhua News Agency was still quoting unnamed "experts" as saying that 90% of Omicron infected people were asymptomatic and mild. Inferring from limited biological knowledge, it is due to the fact that human individuals are confronting the new coronavirus series for the first time, and the immune system has an overreaction.
